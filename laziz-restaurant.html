Our Story

Where Every Meal Is a Celebration

Founded in 2004, Laziz Restaurant has been serving the heart of Gurgaon with authentic North Indian and Mughlai cuisine prepared from generations-old recipes. Our kitchen blends royal Mughal-era cooking techniques with modern presentation.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries the passion of our master chefs who have dedicated decades to perfecting the art of spices. From the first bite of our Butter Chicken to the last spoonful of our Shahi Biryani — we promise a dining experience you'll remember forever.
